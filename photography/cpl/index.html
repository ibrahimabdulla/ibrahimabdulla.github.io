About me

I’ve been working in the industry since 2016 and had the chance to gain experience in both design and development. Over the last years I have worked with both small and large clients like Fujifilm.

I learned that I prefer working on corporate to enterprise projects with more creative freedom, so I joined GO Gulf., a web design and development company in Dubai, where we are passionately working hard to push the web to it’s limits.

Due to my passion for digital design and current trends I applied as a young jury member and am honored to join the Awwwards family and have the chance to vote for the best websites around the world.

I have been always trying to improve myself, grow as a designer, create awesome experiences and solutions.
